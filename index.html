About

I started in IT in my 14 years dismantling and replacing parts of my computer and the family at this time i did some courses such as Hardware, Linux and Digital Prepress (Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign). When I was doing the course of linux I became interested in the programming which made me start to college for Computer Science in 2009. At this time I worked as an intern in the mayor of my town in the area of infrastructure, performing maintenance on the computers. At the end of my internship I conducted a project using the Joomla CMS which made me study and know the best tool for developing a website for one of the sectors of the municipality. I'm currently formed, working four years as a Java developer. This time I had contact with languages like PHP, Visual Basic and C #. In parallel, I am studying and knowing the new features of the language Swift, recently announced by Apple in the future and intend to provide some apps in the App Store and maybe venture into the Android world.
